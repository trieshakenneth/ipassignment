Metamorphosis is a series of major changes in an animals body form as it moves through its life cycle.
Butterflies and moths undergo a complete metamorphosis, which means there are four separate stages in the life cycle (egg, larva, pupa, and adult).
Each stage looks completely different and serves a different purpose in the life of the insect.
